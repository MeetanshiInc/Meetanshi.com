Magento 2 Contactless Delivery

The COVID-19 disease has taken over the world and compelled everyone to practice social distancing. In such a scenario, it is difficult for the online stores to deliver and that too when it comes to essential items delivery, which cannot be avoided, there needs to be a way out.

Meetanshi has developedMagento 2 Contactless Delivery extension that allows the store owners to enable a "no contact" or "contactless delivery" option at the checkout.

The customers can select this option to avail contactless delivery and the delivery person can send them a picture showing the delivered item at the location.

Magento 2 Contactless Delivery extension is what the online store needs to practice social distancing, thrive the business, and fulfil consumers' essential needs at the same time!

Benefits of Magento 2 Contactless Delivery:

  • • The module allows no contact delivery of essential items to practice social distancing.
  • • Show the "Contactless Delivery" option at the checkout page.
  • • Automatically disable COD option when no contact delivery is selected.
  • • Show "a contactless delivery" label on the product page.
  • • Customers can leave a custom message on the checkout page for contactless delivery option.
  • • Allow the delivery person to leave a photo showing that the item is delivered via WhatsApp or Email.
